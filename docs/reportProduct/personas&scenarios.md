### 2.2 Personas and scenarios

#### **Persona 1: Francisco Garcia Gameiro - The Private Field Owner**

**Demographics:**
- Age: 48 years old
- Occupation: Owns a sports complex with 3 padel courts and 2 futsal fields
- Location: Aveiro, Portugal
- Education: High school + business courses
- Tech savviness: Moderate (uses email, basic spreadsheets, WhatsApp)

**Background & Goals:**
Francisco inherited a sports complex from his father. He loves the business but struggles with occupancy rates during weekday afternoons. His main goal is to monetize idle capacity without hiring additional staff to manage bookings. He wants clear visibility into which fields are booked, upcoming maintenance needs, and how much he's earning from each facility.

**Pain Points:**
- Currently manages bookings via WhatsApp messages and phone calls
- Can't easily see which time slots are available at a glance
- No systematic way to track equipment maintenance
- Loses money on empty court time
- Customers forget appointment times and sometimes no-show

**Technology Comfort:**
Francisco is comfortable with smartphone apps and basic online tools, but struggles with complex software. He needs simple, visual interfaces that don't require extensive training.

**Key Quote:**
"I want my courts full and my maintenance tracked. If I could see bookings in a calendar and know when equipment needs service, I'd be happy."

---

**Scenario 1: FRancisco's Monday Morning**

*Time: Monday 9 AM. Francisco is having coffee at his facility and opens SportsLink.*

1. FRancisco opens SportsLink app/web on his phone
2. Lands on "Owner Dashboard" showing his 5 facilities
3. He sees this week's occupancy: Padel courts 65% full, Futsal fields 45% full
4. Notices Padel Court #1 has low occupancy (only 2 bookings for whole week)
5. Clicks on that court and considers lowering the price for this week to attract bookings
6. Checks "Equipment Status" tab: Padel Court #2 has a broken net (flagged from last user review)
7. Schedules maintenance for tomorrow morning and temporarily disables that court
8. Views his earnings chart: made €450 this week, trending +15% from last week
9. Closes app satisfied that his facilities are optimized

**Expected System Interactions:**
- Equipment status updates reflect last user-reported issues
- Price changes take effect within 1 hour for new bookings

- Location: Aveiro, works nearby
- Education: University degree (Marketing)

**Scenario 3: Maria's Thursday Evening**

*Time: Thursday 6 PM. Maria finishes work and decides to play padel. She opens SportsLink.*

3. Clicks "Find a Court" feature
4. Default shows courts within 5km of her location (Aveiro)
5. Filters by "Padel" sport type
6. Filters for availability "Today, 7 PM - 9 PM"
7. Sees 3 results: Padel Club Aveiro (4.8★, €20/hour), Sports Center (4.2★, €18/hour), Academy Pro (4.7★, €22/hour)
8. Clicks on "Padel Club Aveiro" - sees photos, equipment list, reviews, availability calendar
9. Reads 2-3 recent reviews: "Great court!", "Net was excellent", "Owner very responsive"
10. Selects "7 PM - 9 PM" time slot
11. Completes payment (Stripe) in 10 seconds
12. Receives confirmation with address, QR code, parking info
13. Court is now showing as "Booked" for that time slot

**Expected System Interactions:**
- Location detection works smoothly
- Search results load within 1-2 seconds
- Payment completes within 3 seconds
- Confirmation email/SMS received within 30 seconds

**Scenario 4: Pedro Discovers Community on SportsLink**

- Occupancy indicators update in real-time
- Group chat integrates with booking records
- Peer ratings system encourages community engagement

**Key Quote:**

**Scenario 5: Ana's Weekly Operations Review**

*Time: Monday 10 AM. Ana logs into SportsLink Admin Dashboard for her weekly review.*

1. Dashboard loads with real-time KPIs:
   - Active Users: 342 (↑8% week-over-week)
   - Active Facilities: 24
   - This Week's Bookings: 156 (↑12% vs last week)
   - Platform Revenue: €1,240 (30% commission = €372)
   - System Uptime: 99.8%

2. Ana checks "Low Rating Facilities" section
   - Sees "Sports Center Aveiro" dropped to 3.2★ this week
   - Reviews mention: "Court surface worn", "Net broken", "Owner unresponsive"
   - Clicks to view facility owner contact info

3. Sends message to owner: "We've noticed declining reviews about maintenance. Let's schedule a call to address this."

4. Checks "User Complaints" - 3 pending
   - One user reports unpaid deposit refund - escalates to payment team
   - One user reports inappropriate behavior - flags account for warning
   - One user reports facility cleanliness issue - contacts facility owner

5. Views "Growth Analytics" chart
   - Futsal bookings trending highest (+18%)
   - Padel steady (+8%)

6. Exports weekly report for board meeting next Friday

- Dashboard loads all metrics within 2-3 seconds
- Admin can drill into detailed data (facility info, owner contact, booking history)
- Real-time alerts for critical issues (platform down, abuse reported)
- Export functionality creates PDF reports instantly

### **Persona 6: Sofia Mendes - The Casual Group Organizer**

**Demographics:**
- Age: 35 years old
- Occupation: HR Manager
- Location: Lisbon, Portugal
- Education: University degree (HR Management)
- Tech savviness: Moderate to High (expert in social apps, uses calendar and messaging apps extensively)

**Background & Goals:**
Sofia organizes weekly **casual pick-up volleyball games** for her colleagues after work. She is responsible for securing a court and making sure they have the necessary gear (like a quality net or balls, if needed). Her goal is to quickly find an available facility, book a single time slot for the group, and easily add any required equipment rental (like 2 volleyballs) in the same transaction. She needs a simple way to confirm the booking and share the details with her group.

**Pain Points:**
- It's time-consuming to call facilities to check court availability and equipment availability.
- Often, facilities only provide the court, and she has to bring or rent equipment separately.
- Wants a single transaction for both the court and the associated gear.
- Needs clear visibility on the total cost upfront.

**Technology Comfort:**
Sofia is comfortable with online booking and expects the system to be as easy as ordering takeout—fast, clear, and focused on the immediate transaction.

**Key Quote:**
"I just need a decent court and two good balls for Tuesday night. If I can book and pay for both in one place, that's perfect."

---

**Scenario 6 (Revised): Sofia Organizes a Weekly Game**

*Time: Tuesday 1 PM. Sofia is coordinating the evening's game via WhatsApp. She opens SportsLink.*

1. Opens SportsLink app/web and uses the "Find a Facility" search.
2. Filters by "Volleyball," "Lisbon," and "Today, 7 PM - 9 PM."
3. Finds "Lisbon Sports Arena" with an available court.
4. Selects the 7 PM - 9 PM time slot.
5. On the booking page, she sees an optional **Equipment Rental** section associated with the court.
6. She selects: **"Volleyball (High Quality)"** and specifies **Quantity: 2**.
7. The system instantly updates the cost summary: Court (€40) + 2 Volleyballs (€5 each) = **Total €50**.
8. Completes the payment (Credit Card) quickly.
9. Receives a simple confirmation screen with a single, unique link to share with her teammates via WhatsApp. The confirmation clearly lists the court time and the two reserved volleyballs.
10. Closes the app, ready to share the good news with the team.

**Expected System Interactions:**
- Search and filter results are fast and accurate.
- Equipment inventory is tied directly to the selected facility and time slot.
- The cost summary clearly breaks down facility rental and equipment rental.
- Payment is a single, unified transaction for both items.
- Provides a simple, shareable confirmation link/message.
